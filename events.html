Upcoming Events

Find organic and sustainable farming events happening around the UK. These listings are summaries based on public information – always check the organiser’s website for the latest details.

Organic Growers Gathering 2025

Date: 22–24 August 2025

Location: Hardwick Estate, South Oxfordshire

This three‑day gathering brings together organic and small‑scale growers from across the UK. Expect farm tours, skill‑sharing sessions and talks on regenerative agriculture. It’s a great chance to learn and network with other growers.

BFREPA Live 2025

Date: March 2025 (exact dates TBC)

Location: Birmingham National Exhibition Centre

The British Free Range Egg Producers Association hosts its annual show with seminars, equipment displays and panel discussions. It’s geared towards poultry farmers but open to anyone interested in free‑range egg production.

CropTec Show 2026

Date: November 2026

Location: East of England Showground, Peterborough

An exhibition focusing on arable farming and crop management. The show features technical seminars and stands on precision farming, soil health and crop protection. While not exclusively organic, many sessions address sustainable practices.

LAMMA Show 2026

Date: January 2026

Location: National Exhibition Centre, Birmingham

The UK’s largest agricultural machinery show. Discover the latest tractors, tools and technology for farming. Even if you’re focused on small‑scale organic production, the show offers insights into equipment and innovation.
